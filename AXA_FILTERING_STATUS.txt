USAGE
================================================================================

Basic Usage:
  python3 api/filter_axa_startups_enhanced.py \
    --min-score 35 \
    --stats \
    --output downloads/axa_candidates.json

With LLM Validation (slower, more thorough):
  python3 api/filter_axa_startups_enhanced.py \
    --min-score 40 \
    --include-llm-analysis \
    --stats \
    --output downloads/axa_llm_validated.json

Filter Specific Rule:
  python3 api/filter_axa_startups_enhanced.py \
    --min-score 40 \
    --rule 1 \
    --output downloads/axa_platform_enablers.json

Export to CSV:
  python3 api/filter_axa_startups_enhanced.py \
    --min-score 35 \
    --csv \
    --output-dir downloads/axa_export

================================================================================
SCORING SYSTEM
================================================================================

Total Score: 0-100 (normalized from 0-125 raw)

Components:
  Rule Matching:      0-35 points (business model fit)
  Multi-Rule Bonus:   0-10 points (companies matching 2+ rules)
  Funding Score:      0-40 points (maturity & resources)
  Company Size:       0-30 points (capability & stability)
  Maturity:           0-10 points (growth stage)

Tier Classification:
  Tier 1: Must Meet      → funding ≥$100M AND 2+ rules
  Tier 2: High Priority  → funding ≥$50M OR (funding ≥$10M AND 2+ rules) OR score ≥60
  Tier 3: Medium         → score ≥40
  Tier 4: Low            → score ≥20
  Excluded              → score <20
